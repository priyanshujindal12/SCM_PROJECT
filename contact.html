Frequently Asked Questions

Is Guardian Angel available internationally?

Yes, Guardian Angel can be used worldwide, though some features like emergency services integration may vary by location.

How do I set up my emergency contacts?

You can add emergency contacts in the Dashboard section after creating an account. We recommend adding at least 3 trusted contacts.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption and follow strict privacy protocols to ensure your personal information and location data remain secure.
